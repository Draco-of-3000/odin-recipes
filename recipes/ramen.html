Naruto Ichiraku Ramen

Description

Naruto Ichiraku Ramen (ラーメン一楽): Ichiraku's Ramen is Naruto Uzumaki's favorite food. He eats it almost always and usually stops by Ichiraku's dining establishment between missions and he is often joined by Iruka Umino, his sensei. Because Naruto has been a loyal customer for most of his life, Teuchi and his daughter, Ayame, were among the few people in Konoha not to treat Naruto with animosity during his youth.

Ingredients

Ramen Ingredients
  • Japanese ramen
  • 2 pcs boneless chicken thighs (to substitute pork)
  • 2 eggs
  • Narutomaki
  • Seaweed
  • Bamboo shoot
  • Spring onion (for garnish)
  • Bamboo leaves string or any string (to tie up the chicken)
Marinade Sauce Ingredients
  • 400ml water from boiled chicken
  • 80ml soy sauce
  • 4 Tablespoons of vinegar
  • 4 Tablespoons of oyster sauce
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 1 spring onion
  • 1 Tablespoon of black pepper
Ramen Broth Ingredients
  • 1 liter water
  • Leftover water from boiled chicken
  • 3 pcs shitake mushroom
  • 3 spring onion
  • Half yellow onion
  • B4 garlic cloves
  • 50ml fresh milk
  • 2 Tablespoons of fish sauce
  • 2 Tablespoons of miso paste
  • 1 Teaspoon brown sugar
  • 1 chicken cube stock
  • Salt & pepper as desired to taste

Instructions

  1. First, let's prepare the chicken. Wash and clean boneless chicken thigh and roll it over. Tie up using any kind of string.
  2. Use bamboo leaves string and tie using butcher knot technique.
  3. Boil water in a pot and put the chicken in. Let it boil under medium-heat for around 40 - 50 minutes. Once done, take out the chicken and let it cool down. Do not throw away the water from boiled chicken. Set aside.
  4. Next, boil 2 eggs for around 8 - 10 minutes. If you prefer runny yolk, you can boil for 6 - 7 minutes. After boil, transfer the egg into a bowl full of ice to cool it down. Then, peel off the hard-boiled eggs skin. Set aside.
  5. Now, let's prepare the marinade sauce. Put all the marinade sauce ingredients in a pot and cook it under medium-heat for 8 - 10 minutes. Let it cool down for 15 - 20 minutes.
  6. Place the chickens and hard-boiled eggs into the marinade sauce. Marinade for at least 3 - 4 hours or overnight.
  7. Take out the chickens and fry for around 5 - 8 minutes until it dried up. Cool it down then cut off the string and chop into pieces. The shape will remain circular. Set aside.
  8. Take out the marinated hard-boiled eggs and cut into 2. Set aside.
  9. Next, put all the ramen broth ingredients into a big pot and let it cook for around 10 - 15 minutes. Add in around 2 - 4 TBS of the marinade sauce to enhance the taste. Ramen broth is done. Set aside.
  10. Cut the narutomaki, bamboo shoots and spring onion.
  11. Boil narutomaki and bamboo shoot for around 3 - 5 minutes and set aside.
  12. Cook the Japanese ramen in boiling water for 2 - 3 minutes. Next, transfer it into a cool water to cool down.
  13. It's time to assemble our Naruto Ichiraku ramen! Put the ramen noodle inside a big bowl. Pour some ramen broth inside. Add in eggs, narutomaki, bamboo shoots, chickens and some seaweeds. All done!
